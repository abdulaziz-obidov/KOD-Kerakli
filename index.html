The Studio

We are a collective of architects, designers, and thinkers committed to creating spaces that elevate the human experience.

Founded in 2015, ARCHITEKT has grown from a small practice to an internationally recognized firm. Our work spans residential, commercial, and cultural projects across Europe.

We believe in the power of simplicity, the beauty of natural materials, and the importance of sustainable design principles.

Design Philosophy

Grid & Order

Every line serves a purpose. Our designs follow strict geometric principles rooted in Swiss design tradition.

Material Truth

We celebrate natural materials in their honest state—concrete, wood, steel, and glass.

Sustainable Future

Environmental responsibility is not optional. We integrate passive design and renewable systems.
